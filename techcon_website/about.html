Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small local gathering of passionate developers and technologists. Over the years, it has grown into an internationally recognized conference, drawing thousands of attendees and hundreds of speakers each year.

Our Mission

At TechCon, we aim to empower innovators, foster inclusive technology communities, and ignite conversations that shape the future of tech. We believe in accessible education, ethical innovation, and global collaboration.
